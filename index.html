The hospital offers end-to-end clinical, surgical and diagnostic services. Sigma Health Hospitals offers advanced Endoscopic procedures, Hepatobiliary and Liver Surgeries, Bariatric Surgery and Transplants. The hospital has round-the-clock services for Emergencies, Critical Care and Trauma.

Sigma Health Hospitals is a part of IHH Healthcare, a leading premium integrated healthcare provider with a network of 43 hospitals and more than 16,000 licensed beds.
